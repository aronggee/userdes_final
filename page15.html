12-Column Grid Design

These are examples of 12 by 12 grid design layout for website. You can choose base on the type of website you want to create, it can be just pictures, text or a combination of two. You can also design your own or resize the layout base on the size of your picture or the number of content you wanted to input.
 12 by 12 design layout is easier to design than  3 by 3 or 4 by 4 because there are 12 columns, and by that, you can make use of each boxes to input different designs. Unlike 3x3 and 4x4, you can't maximize the space because you are limited with only 3 or 4 boxes/cells.

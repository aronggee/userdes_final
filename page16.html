MY REFLECTION PAPERS

Reflection 1 : "FIRST WEEK OF USERDES CLASS" 

                                                        
The first week of USERDES class is very fun because starting from the first day, we had lots of activities, and that’s how we introduce ourselves. Activities are fun because it’s subjective, meaning, nothing’s wrong. That’s the kind of activity that I wanted, though, it’s essay form-- because that’s where I’m not really good at. This week, we tackled about what design means, we gave our opinions on what our minds thinking when we heard the word ‘design’. And I learned that what design means is the sole purpose of that thing, and that is why it is formed in that way. We had an activity about how we design an elevator with a thousand floors. My idea is mainly put a tablet-like object outside every elevator for the users to input their desired floor level before entering the elevator. While I was thinking of this proposal, I thought that it would really solve the problem, but when others presented their ideas, I noticed that my proposal is vague and not the best solution for that matter. In this activity, I learned that everything can be improved with the help of the continuous development of the technology and of course the broad critical thinking and idea making of everyone.

 Reflection 2: "Need Finding and Paper Prototype" 

                                                       

In our second week in USERDES, we are challenged to work as a team, and what we have to do is to create an application that will solve the problem that we have thought. Need Finding is the topic that was discussed in this week, it is a process of looking for a solution in the problem, and by that you must fully understand what your problem is for you to provide the best solution that you can make. 


In my group, the problem that we tried to solve is the language barrier, we all know that there are lots of languages, and we can’t practice all of them. So, we planned to create an app that will translate someone’s idea into someone whom he is talking to’s language. Dialect also plays a role in this app, example you want to translate Cebuano to Ilocano. My group and I decided to pursue this app because we believe that a good communication is the way to share precise data or information. The next meeting, we discussed it in front to have our prof’s comment and to know other’s opinion about it. After that, we will create a prototype of the application to have a look of our model, and its features.

I learned that teamwork is important because at first, we can’t think of a problem that can be solved easily, everyone gave their proposal but unfortunately, it is vague and unreasonable. But when we combine the problems that we are proposing, we have thought of this idea, the what ifs helps us to build problem that can possibly solve.  

 Reflection 3: "First Web Application using Github and Heroku" 

On our 3rd week in USERDES, we are given a task to create a webpage where in our Activity 1 will be the content. I have a background in creating a webpage because I had web designing subject back in my Junior high school. I use html instead of sublime because I used to work on it before and it is a good practice for me to see in myself if I still remember what I have learned. I created a simple webpage and didn’t search for a template because I want to showcase my knowledge about it, and I don’t want my professor to have high expectation on me whenever she asked us to create a webpage. So overall, the task wasn’t that challenging that I have to search a lot. But it is a practice for us to have an introduction about designing a webpage because I know that sooner or later, this subject will be our backbone creating a front-end in mobile or web programming. 

I had a hard time using Heroku so I asked help with my classmates but in github, I already knew how to use it since during bootcamp in Senior high school, our professor already introduced it to us.

GRID LAYOUT DESIGN

3 x 3

These are examples of 3 by 3 and 4 by 4 grid design layout for website. You can choose base on the type of website you want to create, it can be just pictures, text or a combination of two. You can also design your own or resize the layout base on the size of your picture or the number of content you wanted to input.

These are the reasons why we should use Grid 

  • Grids bring order to a layout making it easier for visitors to find and navigate through information.
  • Grids allow designers to quickly add elements to a layout because many layout decisions are addressed while building the grid structure.
  • Grids make it easier for other designers to work and collaborate on the design as they provide a plan for where to place elements.
  • Grids lead to consistency in the layout of pages across a single site or even several sites creating a structural harmony in the design.
